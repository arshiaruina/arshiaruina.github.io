### Science Demonstrator
[La Nuit de la Sciences](http://institutions.ville-geneve.ch/fr/mhn/votre-visite/musee-dhistoire-des-sciences/evenements/nuit-de-la-science/)
(2022)

* La Nuit de la Sciences is Geneva's annual public science fair, the 2022 edition saw 30-35k attendees over two days
* I demonstrated the Mini-PAN (prototype of the Penetrating Particle Analyzer) and the TimePix technology as part of the team from the Department of Nuclear and Particle Physics [DPNC](https://www.unige.ch/dpnc/en/) of the University of Geneva

### Visitor Management
[CERN Open Days](https://opendays.cern/)
(2019)

* CERN Open Days are a special event taking place every 4-5 years, during which CERN allows the public to visit its facilities, including normally inaccessible areas like the underground experimental caverns
* The 2019 edition saw 75k attendees (x2 higher than expected)
* I supported visitor management efforts at the ALICE experimental facility entrance
